Privacy Policy

Use of Campaign Data

When you use PIES tools and applications, you may be providing or maintaining in databases maintained in or through PIES, specific information about campaign donors, supporters, volunteers, potential donors, finances and personnel,. All of this information relating to specifically identifiable individuals is referred to a “Campaign Data.”

PCCC will not disclose your Campaign Data to any other person or entity for any purpose whatsoever, other than an employee of or consultant to PCCC for the purpose of maintaining, operating, modifying, and improving PIES. In no event will PCCC disclose your Campaign Data to any other user of PIES . PCCC does reserves the right, however, to include your Campaign Data in aggregate data and analyses, and to disclose such aggregate data and analyses publicly or to such persons and organizations as PCCC deems appropriate.

PCCC also reserves the right to access your Campaign Data for the purpose of monitoring the progress, status and activities of your campaign, and for purposes of carrying out PCCC’s own programs and activities, including but not limiting to determining whether, when, and how PCCC may endorse a candidate and determining the allocation of PCCC’s resources to particular campaign; and for the purpose of modifying and improving PIES.

In addition, PCCC reserves the right to disclose your Campaign Data to companies that assist us in maintaining PIES but only for purposes of providing services to PCCC with the strict requirement that any such company will never itself have the right to use your Campaign Data itself for any reason. PCCC also reserves the right to disclose or use your Campaign Data to comply with applicable law or legal requirements (for example, a lawful subpoena), to protect our rights or property, or to protect PCCC supporters from fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful conduct.

Upon termination of your access to PIES by PCCC for any reason, you may request a copy of your Campaign Data by sending an e-mail to pies@boldprogressives.org. PCCC will make reasonable efforts to provide to you as much of your Campaign Data as practicable. PCCC may also retain its own copy of your Campaign Data for use and analysis by PCCC internally.

Children

PCCC complies with the Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act. PCCC does not solicit and does not accept personally identifying information from any person under 13 years of age. Visitors to PIES who are under 13 years of age should not use this Site except under the supervision and with the assistance of their parent or legal guardian, and should never submit any personally identifying information to the Site. For example, visitors under 13 years of age should not use services or take any actions on or through PIES.

Links to Other Sites

The privacy policies and practices contained in this Privacy Statement do not apply to ANY external links. This Privacy Statement only applies to PIES or any future sites that we may develop. It does not cover sites that are linked to by PIES or sites for which we are not responsible ("Linked Sites"). These Linked Sites will have their own policies and practices which may be different from ours. We therefore encourage you to familiarize yourself with the policies and practices of the Linked Sites, especially if you provide personal information to them.

California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

PCCC complies with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. As part of that Act, all users of PIES may make any changes to their information at any time by logging into their Control Panel and going to the “Edit Profile” page.

Amendments and Consent to This Privacy Statement

We retain the right to amend or otherwise update this Privacy Statement at any time. By using PIES, you consent to the collection and use of the information as we have described. If we change our policies and practices, we will post the changes in our Privacy Statement so that you are always aware of them. With this knowledge, you can make an informed decision about whether you wish to provide personal information to us.
